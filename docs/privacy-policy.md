# Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-02-22

This Privacy Policy explains how this SMS appointment assistant collects, uses, and protects personal information.

## Information We Collect

When users interact with the SMS service, we may collect:

- Phone number
- Message content
- Appointment details (requested time, confirmation, cancellation, reschedule activity)
- Message metadata (timestamps, delivery status, provider message IDs)

## How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

- Respond to incoming SMS messages
- Schedule, confirm, reschedule, and cancel appointments
- Send appointment reminders
- Maintain service reliability, security, and abuse prevention
- Meet legal and compliance requirements for messaging

## SMS Program Disclosures

- Users opt in by texting the business number first.
- By texting, users consent to receive appointment and scheduling-related SMS messages.
- Message frequency varies by user activity.
- Message and data rates may apply.
- Users can reply `STOP` to opt out and `HELP` for help.

## Sharing of Information

We do not sell personal information.

We share limited information only with service providers required to operate this service, such as:

- SMS delivery providers (for message routing and delivery)
- Cloud/database infrastructure providers (for secure hosting and storage)

Providers are expected to process data only as needed to deliver the service.

## Data Retention

Message and appointment records are retained for operational, audit, and support purposes, and may be deleted when no longer needed or when required by applicable law.

## Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect data. No system is completely secure, but we work to reduce risk and protect user information.

## User Choices

- Reply `STOP` to stop SMS messages.
- Reply `HELP` for support information.
- Users may request updates or deletion of their contact information where applicable.

## Contact

For privacy questions, contact the business support channel associated with this SMS service.
