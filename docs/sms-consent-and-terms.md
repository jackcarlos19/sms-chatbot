# SMS Consent and Messaging Terms

This page documents consent and messaging disclosures for the SMS appointment assistant used by this project.

## Program Description

Users can text this business to schedule, confirm, reschedule, or cancel appointments.
The service sends conversational SMS responses related to appointment management.

## Consent and Opt-In

- Users initiate messaging by texting the business number first.
- By texting the business number, users consent to receive scheduling-related SMS messages.
- Message and data rates may apply.
- Message frequency varies based on user interaction and appointment activity.

## Opt-Out and Help

- Reply `STOP` at any time to opt out of SMS messages.
- Reply `HELP` for help or support information.

## Example Messages

- User: "I'd like to book an appointment."
- System: "Here are some available times... Reply with a number to book."
- System: "Great, should I book Monday at 10:00 AM? Reply YES to confirm."
- System: "Reminder: You have an appointment tomorrow..."

## Support

For support, users can reply `HELP` or contact the business support line.
